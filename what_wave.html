How to Pick Your Wave


Swell:

Term for refering to the series of waves during a certain period of time. More experienced surfers prefer a "bigger swell" (aka: bigger waves).




Tide:

Many surfers prefer low tide, but tide is often an individual preference.




Wind:

Essentially all surfers prefer little or no wind, at least on the coast.




Wave Size (ft):

Beginners: 2-3

Intermediate: 5-7

Advanced: 7+
